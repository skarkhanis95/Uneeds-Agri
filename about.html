Overview

Uneeds Systems LLP is a forward-thinking agriculture company headquartered in Pune, India. Established in 2021, the company is dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural supply chain by eliminating intermediaries, fostering direct connections between farmers and distributors, wholesalers, and markets. By leveraging a farmer-first approach and modern practices, Uneeds Systems LLP ensures fair pricing for farmers and high-quality produce for buyers.
